Are You Going With Me?

The North Sea Jazz Festival / 2003

Pat Metheny : Guitar
Jim McNeely : Conductor
The Metropole Orchestra

"Are You Going With Me?" is a song by Pat Metheny, composed by Metheny and Lyle Mays. First recorded on the Pat Metheny Group's third album, Offramp (1982), and played live by the Pat Metheny Group ever since, it has become a "timeless" trademark Metheny song, his "musical signature."

The North Sea Jazz Festival is an annual jazz festival held each second weekend of July in the Netherlands at the Ahoy venue. It used to be in The Hague but since 2006 it has been held in Rotterdam. This is because the Statenhal where the festival was held before was demolished in 2006.
